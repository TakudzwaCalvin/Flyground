About us

It's all about construction

Flyground is a fully licensed limited liability company founded in 2020 as full painting services and other construction services. We are servicing the Kwa-Zulu Natal area including but not limited to Pietermaritzburg, Durban, Pinetown, and many more.

Although the company is small in operations it is not without a solid foundation. The company is owned by experienced entrepreneurs; Shelton has three years in digital designing and information technology and Kelly is an expert in financial analysis, business development, and Marketing research. The combination of expertise goes hand in hand with business success and development.
